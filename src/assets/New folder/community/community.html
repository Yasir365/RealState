Community

At REAL, we believe our strength lies in the active involvement of our community members. Whether you’re a token holder, developer, crypto enthusiast, or just exploring, REAL offers a unique space for everyone to learn, connect, and make a tangible impact on the platform’s growth.

Why Join the REAL Community?

Play an Active Role in REAL’s Future

Joining the REAL community means more than just holding tokens—it’s about being an essential part of a project that’s reshaping real estate through blockchain. Token holders are invited to actively participate in REAL’s governance, helping make decisions that guide our growth and long-term vision.

Shape the Platform Through Governance

As a REAL token holder, you’re a valued member of our Decentralized Autonomous Organization (DAO). Through the DAO, each holder can submit ideas, discuss important topics, and vote on key decisions, from property acquisitions to platform enhancements.

Connect and Collaborate with Like-Minded Members

The REAL community is made up of people who share a commitment to innovation, transparency, and decentralized decision-making. Connect with a network of individuals who bring diverse insights and experiences, from blockchain beginners to real estate professionals.

How to Get Involved

Participate Actively in Governance

As a DAO member, each token holder has the power to propose and vote on changes. With every 1,000 REAL tokens representing one vote, members are empowered to shape REAL’s future collectively.

Engage with the Community on Social Media

REAL thrives on open communication and collaboration. Join us on Discord, Telegram, and other social platforms to stay informed, ask questions, and engage with fellow members.

Bring Your Ideas and Contribute to REAL’s Growth

Got an idea for a new feature or property opportunity? The REAL platform encourages its community members to contribute ideas that will drive our progress.

A Community That Grows With You

At REAL, we’re not just building a platform—we’re creating a dynamic ecosystem powered by its members. The REAL community is a place to learn, connect, and contribute to something meaningful.
