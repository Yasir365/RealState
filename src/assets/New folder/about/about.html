About Us

Who We Are

Welcome to REAL—the Real Estate Alliance League—a community-driven real estate DAO that redefines access to property holdings. Built on the Ethereum blockchain, REAL empowers individuals to actively participate in a transparent, secure, and decentralized ecosystem, blending blockchain innovation with real-world real estate opportunities. Our members aren’t just token holders; they’re a core part of our decision-making community. With REAL tokens, holders can influence and interact with the project’s growth, benefiting from both a tokenized economy and tangible real estate property opportunities.

Our Mission

REAL’s mission is to make real estate investment accessible, rewarding, and community-focused. By using the Ethereum blockchain’s security and transparency, we provide a platform that builds passive income through rental properties.

The income from rental properties fuels our deflationary model, enhancing token value by reducing supply through “Token Burns.” This process creates a long-term, member-driven approach to value within a fixed supply of 100 million tokens.

Our members shape REAL’s future through a DAO structure, ensuring that each voice within the community is heard.

What Makes REAL Unique

  • Transparent & Decentralized: Built on Ethereum’s secure and transparent framework.
  • Cost-Free Membership: No membership fees; benefits grow with token holdings.
  • Deflationary Token Model: Capped supply with regular token burns ensures long-term value.
  • Active Governance: Members participate in decisions on upgrades, disbursements, and leadership roles.

The REAL Platform

  • Holdings Transparency: Track rental property records and financials.
  • Property Submission: Members can suggest new properties for review.
  • Governance and Voting: Members influence key decisions on disbursement, upgrades, and leadership roles.
  • Community Engagement: Real-time discussions with members, sharing insights.
  • Document Access: Access templates for property submissions and proposals.
  • Continuous Token Burn: Profits buy and burn tokens, reducing supply and increasing value.
