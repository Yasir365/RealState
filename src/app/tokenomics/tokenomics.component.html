Token Supply and Distribution

Maximum Token Supply

REAL has a fixed maximum supply of 100 million tokens, meaning no additional tokens will ever be created. This limited supply reinforces token exclusivity over time.

Token Allocation

The distribution of tokens ensures support for project goals, community engagement, and platform growth:

  • Founders and Team: 4% tokens are allocated to core contributors to ensure long-term project development.
  • Start-Up and Development: 2% tokens support platform technology and growth efforts.
  • Liquidity Pool Burn: 2% tokens will be added to Liquidity Pool and LP tokens will be burned ensuring security and confidence from our community.
  • Community Engagement: 92% tokens are allocated for phases of property acquisition, expanding opportunities as the REAL platform grows.

How the Deflationary Model Works

The REAL token’s deflationary model is designed to make membership increasingly exclusive as the token supply is reduced over time. Here’s how the model works:

Rental Income and Token Buybacks

All rental profits generated by REAL properties are used to buy REAL tokens from the open market.

Token Burn Process:

Once purchased, these tokens are permanently removed or “burned,” which reduces the total circulating supply.

Increased Exclusivity

With each token burn, the remaining tokens become more exclusive, as each token represents a larger portion of the platform’s resources and benefits.This approach not only supports long-term engagement but also ensures that token holders benefit from ongoing demand and scarcity.

REAL’s Passive Income Strategy

Our platform generates passive income through rental properties, which is then reinvested to benefit the entire community. Here’s a breakdown:

Sustained Income

Income from rental properties is continuous, providing a steady flow of resources back to the platform.

Funding Token Burns

This income fuels ongoing buybacks and token burns, reinforcing the deflationary model.

Membership Benefits

As token burns continue, REAL tokens become more exclusive, enhancing the platform experience for each member.

Market Dynamics and Token Value

Deflationary Mechanism
