GOVERNANCE

At REAL, we are committed to creating a truly decentralized, community-driven platform that empowers members to shape the future of real estate. By utilizing a Decentralized Autonomous Organization (DAO) structure, we ensure that every member has a voice in guiding the project’s development and initiatives.

Decentralized Autonomous Organization (DAO)

The REAL DAO enables token holders to actively influence and contribute to the direction of the project. By joining the DAO, members gain a unique opportunity to engage with the REAL community, propose new ideas, and vote on important decisions.

Shape the Platform’s Future

Influence key decisions, from new property acquisitions to platform upgrades.

Propose Platform Enhancements

Suggest ideas and improvements to make the REAL platform more engaging and effective.

Participate in Governance Discussions

Join conversations that drive REAL’s direction, working directly with other members to ensure informed decision-making.

Increase Membership Exclusivity

With each token burn, the supply decreases, enhancing membership exclusivity and making the REAL community a distinctive space.

Non-Profit Organization

To support REAL’s goals, a non-profit organization works alongside the DAO, handling critical operational tasks like research, management, and overall project development.

Responsibilities

The non-profit ensures that all DAO decisions are implemented effectively, maintaining alignment with the community’s vision and priorities.

Election Mechanism

Every four years, DAO members participate in electing officials for the non-profit, reinforcing a democratic, community-oriented approach.

Governance Components

REAL’s governance structure includes multiple components to ensure a balanced, participatory approach.

Governance Proposals

Any DAO member can submit a proposal to introduce new features, allocate funds, or suggest improvements.

Non-Profit Representation

The non-profit upholds the best interests of token holders, ensuring all DAO decisions are ethically and transparently implemented.

Governance Discussions

Once a proposal is submitted, it enters a discussion period where DAO members can offer feedback and insights.

Governance Voting

After the discussion period, eligible token holders vote on proposals, with each 1,000 REAL tokens representing one vote.

Decentralized Decision-Making

The REAL governance model empowers members to take part in key decision-making processes and is guided by the following principles:

Transparency

All proposals, discussions, and voting outcomes are publicly accessible, fostering accountability and building trust within the community.

Inclusivity

Every REAL token holder has the opportunity to participate in governance, regardless of their stake.

Adaptability

The REAL governance framework is designed to evolve in response to the community’s needs and the blockchain landscape.
