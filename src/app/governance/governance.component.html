DAO Membership Benefits

Non-Profit Organization

  • Responsibilities: Handles operational tasks, ensuring smooth implementation of DAO decisions.
  • Election Mechanism: Members elect officials every four years, ensuring democratic leadership.

Governance Components

  • Proposals: Any DAO member can submit proposals for platform features or resource allocation.
  • Representation: The non-profit ensures transparent implementation of DAO decisions.
  • Discussion Period: Members provide feedback, ensuring well-rounded consideration before voting.
  • Voting: Each 1,000 REAL tokens represent one vote, encouraging active participation.

Decentralized Decision-Making

  • Transparency: All proposals, discussions, and outcomes are accessible to foster accountability.
  • Inclusivity: Every REAL token holder can participate, ensuring diverse and fair representation.
  • Adaptability: Our governance model evolves to meet community needs and blockchain advancements.
