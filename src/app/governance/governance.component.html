GOVERNANCE

At REAL, we are committed to creating a truly decentralized, community-driven platform that empowers members to shape the future of real estate. By utilizing a Decentralized Autonomous Organization (DAO) structure, we ensure that every member has a voice in guiding the project’s development and initiatives.

Decentralized Autonomous Organization (DAO)

The REAL DAO enables token holders to actively influence and contribute to the direction of the project. By joining the DAO, members gain a unique opportunity to engage with the REAL community, propose new ideas, and vote on important decisions. Membership in the DAO requires a deposit of 1,000 REAL tokens, with each 1,000 tokens representing one vote. This system encourages long-term engagement and participation, with more voting power available to members holding a larger stake in the community.
Our governance structure is designed to provide meaningful opportunities for involvement, giving each member the ability to impact REAL’s evolution and direction.

DAO Membership Benefits

Membership in the REAL DAO provides exclusive benefits, making each member an integral part of the platform. As a DAO member, you can:

Shape the Platform’s Future:

Influence key decisions, from new property acquisitions to platform upgrades.

Propose Platform Enhancements

Suggest ideas and improvements to make the REAL platform more engaging and effective.

Participate in Exclusive Governance Discussions

Join conversations that drive REAL’s direction, working directly with other members to ensure informed decision-making.

REAL’s DAO structure allows members to not only contribute but also to experience a growing level of exclusivity, benefiting from our deflationary model as tokens are burned over time.

Non-Profit Organization

To support REAL’s goals, a non-profit organization works alongside the DAO, handling critical operational tasks like research, management, and overall project development. Here’s how it functions

Responsibilities

The non-profit ensures that all DAO decisions are implemented effectively, maintaining alignment with the community’s vision and priorities. This layer of support allows DAO members to focus on big-picture decisions while the non-profit manages day-to-day execution.

Election Mechanism

Every four years, DAO members participate in electing officials for the non-profit, reinforcing a democratic, community-oriented approach. This process ensures that organizational leadership remains aligned with community interests and adapts to the evolving needs of the project.

Governance Components

REAL’s governance structure includes multiple components to ensure a balanced, participatory approach

Governance Proposals

Any DAO member can submit a proposal to introduce new features, allocate funds, or suggest improvements. Proposals may include ideas such as additional property types, new platform features, or strategic partnerships to expand REAL’s reach and value.

Non-Profit Representation

The non-profit upholds the best interests of token holders, ensuring all DAO decisions are ethically and transparently implemented. This layer of representation adds an additional level of accountability and consistency to our governance model.

Governance Discussions

Once a proposal is submitted, it enters a discussion period where DAO members can offer feedback, share concerns, and contribute insights. This collaborative approach ensures that each proposal receives comprehensive consideration before moving forward.

Governance Voting

After the discussion period, eligible token holders vote on proposals. With each 1,000 REAL tokens representing one vote, members play an active role in deciding which ideas become part of the REAL platform. A majority consensus determines the outcome, ensuring fair representation.

Decentralized Decision-Making

The REAL governance model empowers members to take part in key decision-making processes and is guided by the following principles:

Transparency

All proposals, discussions, and voting outcomes are publicly accessible, fostering accountability and building trust within the community. Members can review governance actions and outcomes through regular reports, providing a clear view into the platform’s ongoing development.

Inclusivity

Every REAL token holder has the opportunity to participate in governance, regardless of their stake. This inclusivity ensures that all voices are heard and valued, creating a diverse, representative community that shapes REAL’s direction.

Adaptability

The REAL governance framework is designed to evolve in response to the community’s needs and the blockchain landscape. By remaining flexible, REAL can adapt to emerging opportunities, ensuring relevance and resilience as the project grows.
