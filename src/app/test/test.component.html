Who We Are

Welcome to REAL—the Real Estate Alliance League—a community-driven real estate DAO that redefines access to property holdings.

Built on the Ethereum blockchain, REAL empowers individuals to actively participate in a transparent, secure, and decentralized ecosystem, blending blockchain innovation with real-world real estate opportunities.

Our members aren’t just token holders; they’re a core part of our decision-making community. With REAL tokens, holders can influence and interact with the project’s growth, benefiting from both a tokenized economy and tangible real estate property opportunities.

Our Mission

REAL’s mission is to make real estate investment accessible, rewarding, and community-focused. By using the Ethereum blockchain’s security and transparency, we provide a platform that builds passive income through rental properties.

The income from rental properties fuels our deflationary model, which enhances token value by regularly reducing supply through “Token Burns.” This process creates a long-term, member-driven approach to value and exclusivity, all within a fixed supply of 100 million tokens.

Our members shape REAL’s future through a DAO structure, making decisions on everything from project initiatives to leadership, ensuring that each voice within the community is heard.

What Makes REAL Unique

Transparent & Decentralized

REAL is built on the Ethereum blockchain, leveraging its security and transparent framework to deliver a community-driven investment experience.

Cost-Free Membership

REAL has no membership fees—just hold REAL tokens to access all platform features, with membership benefits increasing alongside token holdings.

Deflationary Token Model

With a capped supply and regular token burns, REAL’s deflationary model sustains long-term value and aligns with the growth of the real estate portfolio.

Active Governance

The DAO gives members direct involvement, from voting on platform upgrades to deciding on token disbursement and even leadership choices, making REAL a truly collaborative project.

The REAL Platform

The REAL platform is designed to provide comprehensive tools and resources, giving members access to detailed information, governance rights, and a collaborative community environment.
Here are the platform’s core use cases:

Holdings Transparency

Members can view rental property records and financials, allowing them to track the real-world properties generating revenue for the platform. This level of transparency builds trust and provides insight into property performance.

Property Submission and Review

Members can submit new property suggestions for review, contributing to the platform’s real estate portfolio expansion. Each submission is carefully reviewed, allowing the community to help shape future opportunities.

Governance and Voting

REAL token holders can participate in voting on key aspects of the platform. Members have a say in:

  • Token market disbursement and adjustments
  • Platform upgrades, improvements, and new features
  • Leadership roles and decision-making positions within the platform

Community Engagement

REAL’s platform fosters an active and interactive community. Members have access to a community chat built to engage in real-time discussions with other members, share insights, and stay informed about platform updates.

Document and Template Access

The platform provides members with a library of document templates, making it easier for users to submit property information, propose ideas, and stay organized. This feature simplifies the submission process and ensures that everyone has access to standardized forms for efficient communication.

Continuous Token Burn Mechanism

Through profits from rental properties, the organization buys REAL tokens from the open market and burns them, reducing supply and supporting token value. This feature not only increases the exclusivity of the platform but also adds a uniquelong-term value proposition for all token holders.
