What is REAL

Welcome to the Real Estate Alliance League, – a community-driven real estate Decentralized Autonomous Organization or DAO, where you can interact, learn, and contribute to the health and success of our organization and its community.

REAL is a deflationary crypto utility token utilizing the Ethereum blockchain technology bringing the security and transparency of its network to our members. There are no membership fees. You become a member simply by holding REAL tokens. The more tokens held, the higher your membership level.

Profits from the token sales are invested into real estate rental properties. The organization then generates passive income that will continue for the entire lifetime of the property.

This passive income is then used to buy REAL tokens from the open market and eliminate them with a process called “Token Burn”. This will create a lifetime of buying pressure for the token. This act will continue to make the membership more exclusive over the years beyond its initial market cap of 100 million tokens.

Join a transparent, secure, and community-driven project that uses the latest blockchain technology all while being powered by a supportive and active community.
